Alash Orda

ALASH ORDA (KAZAKH: АЛАШ ОРДА, ROMANIZED: ALAŞ ORDA; RUSSIAN: АЛАШ-ОРДА, ROMANIZED: ALASH-ORDA) WAS A KAZAKH PROVISIONAL GOVERNMENT, OR PROTO-STATE, LOCATED MAINLY IN CENTRAL ASIA, AND PARTLY IN EASTERN EUROPE. IT WAS PART OF THE RUSSIAN REPUBLIC, AND THEN SOVIET RUSSIA.
